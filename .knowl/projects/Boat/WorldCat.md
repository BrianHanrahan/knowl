# 2011 World Cat 290 DC

## Overview

The World Cat 290 DC (Dual Console) is a semi-displacement power catamaran built by World Class Catamarans in Tarboro, North Carolina. It is designed as a versatile family fishing and deck boat, combining offshore fishing capability with family-friendly comfort. The dual-console layout, VectorFlo catamaran hulls, and wave-breaking tunnel give it a notably stable, soft, and dry ride compared to traditional monohull boats.

## Key Features

### Hull & Construction

- **VectorFlo semi-displacement catamaran hulls** — stable, soft, and dry ride
- **Unibody construction** — foam and fibreglass stringers in a complex grid for offshore durability
- **Closed moulding** on hatches and components — flawless finish inside and out
- Wave-breaking tunnel for reduced slamming in chop

### Layout

- **Dual console design** with bi-fold weather door between consoles
- **Forward cockpit** — large sun lounge, seating for 4–6, rod storage (up to 6 rods), fishbox
- **Port console** — standup head with stainless steel sink and full standing room
- **Starboard console** — cockpit table, battery access (3 banks), holding tank access, spacious enough for an airbed overnight
- **Aft cockpit** — huge open space with 6 gunwale-mounted rod holders, 6 pop-up cleats, raw-water washdown
- **Euro transom** — walkthrough platform between twin outboards for easy aft access; great for hauling in fish
- Removable seat cushions and table convert from fishing vessel to family cruiser

### Fishing Features

- Live well
- Fresh and salt water washdowns
- Multiple rod holders (gunwale + hardtop rocket launchers if T-top equipped)
- Large insulated fishbox/cooler (175 L)
- Auxiliary fishbox (212 L forward)
- Euro transom for aft fishing access

### Electronics / Navigation

- Garmin GPS
- Garmin Autopilot
- Garmin Radar
- Garmin Transducer
- 3-piece tinted safety glass windscreen

### Other Standard Equipment

- 3-bank battery charger + windlass
- Electro-hydraulic steering
- Pop-up fender cleats
- Moulded bowsprit (keeps anchor line away from topsides)
- Deep ground tackle compartments
- Optional fibreglass T-top with polished aluminium frame

## Highs & Lows (from reviews)

### ✅ Highs

- Brilliant entertaining and social platform
- Flawless mouldings and finish
- Soft, dry ride — even in beam seas and boat wakes
- Excellent stability at rest and underway
- Hands-off downwind tracking
- Quiet cockpit at WOT (open design doesn't trap engine noise)
- Rock-solid stability at anchor

### ⚠️ Lows

- Better suited to summer/day use — very open boat
- Not ideal for extended overnight cruising
- Nav lights considered undersized by some reviewers
