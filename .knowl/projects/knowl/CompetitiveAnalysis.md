# Knowl vs. Claude Cowork — Competitive Summary

## Cowork in one line
Autonomous task executor for your local files and workflows. Powerful, but a black box.

## Knowl's key differentiators
- **Visible context** — users can see, edit, and manage what AI knows about them
- **Voice-first** — hands-free, conversational interface
- **Portable identity** — context travels across sessions and tools, not tied to one machine
- **"Know me" vs. "do my work"** — Knowl owns the personal context/identity lane; Cowork owns task execution

## Strategic framing
Cowork is a consumer of context. Knowl *creates and manages* it. As AI task tools proliferate, a trusted personal context layer becomes more valuable, not less.
